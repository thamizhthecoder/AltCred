How It Works

ALT-CRED is a simple platform which helps to increase your possibilities to get a loan in 3 simple steps

Step 1: Join AltCred

Effortless Onboarding:Sign up using your smartphone without extensive paperwork.

Establish Your Digital Identity: Create your profile and invite trusted community members to endorse your credibility.

Step 2: Get Approved Using Your Transaction History & Community Data

Leverage Your Bank-Linked UPI Transactions: With your consent, AltCred securely accesses your UPI transaction history—which requires a linked bank account—along with your mobile recharge and bill payment records to assess your financial behavior.

Holistic Credit Assessment: These real-world financial insights are combined with community endorsements to create a comprehensive AltCred Score that truly reflects your trustworthiness.

Step 3: Get Approved and Access Nearby Banks

Seamless Loan Approval: Use your AltCred Score to apply for microloans directly through the app.

Broaden Your Financial Options: Once approved, approach partnered local banks and financial institutions, showcasing your enhanced profile and trust score to secure competitive loan terms.
